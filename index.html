Pasta recipes

Boil the required pasta in a small saucepan and cook over medium-low heat until pasta boiled.
After 5 minutes, place the boiled pasta in a saucepan with adding some oil to it.You can use any oil like mustard oil or olive oil.Add chopped onion and tomatoes also with extra garlic paste.You can also add eggs for some extra taste. Stir fry the pasta. Season with salt, pepper and paprika. Drain and place on a sserving platter. Spoon sauce on top and sprinkle with parsley and sesame seed. Enjoy!

Ingredients Required

  • 1 Pack(20 oz) of Pasta
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 small red onion,chopped fine
  • 2 small or three tomatoes, also chopped
  • 2 tbsp italian tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
